Cookies Policy

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better user experience and analyze how our site is used.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off:

  • Session cookies: Maintain your login status and form data
  • Security cookies: Protect against fraud and security threats
  • Load balancing cookies: Ensure optimal site performance

Functional Cookies

These cookies enhance your experience but are not essential:

  • Preference cookies: Remember your settings and preferences
  • Language cookies: Store your language selection
  • Form completion cookies: Save progress on application forms

Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our site:

  • Google Analytics: Track page views, user behavior, and site performance
  • Conversion tracking: Measure the effectiveness of our services
  • Heat mapping: Understand user interaction patterns

Marketing Cookies

These cookies are used for advertising and marketing purposes (with consent):

  • Social media cookies: Enable sharing and social media integration
  • Advertising cookies: Deliver relevant advertisements
  • Remarketing cookies: Show targeted content to previous visitors

3. Cookie Duration

  • Session cookies: Deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent cookies: Remain for a set period (typically 30 days to 2 years)

4. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through:

  • Browser settings: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies
  • Cookie consent banner: Adjust preferences when you first visit our site
  • Opt-out tools: Use industry standard opt-out mechanisms

5. Third-Party Cookies

We use third-party services that may set their own cookies:

  • Google Analytics: Google Privacy Policy
  • Social media platforms: LinkedIn, Facebook, Twitter privacy policies apply
  • Payment processors: Secure payment handling with appropriate privacy protections

6. Contact Us About Cookies

If you have questions about our use of cookies, please contact us at cookies@educonnect.asia
