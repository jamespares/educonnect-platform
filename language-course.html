Personal Pronouns

汉字 Pinyin English Audio
我 wǒ I / me
你 nǐ You
您 nín You (polite)
他 tā He / him
她 tā She / her
我们 wǒmen We / us
你们 nǐmen You all
他们 tāmen They / them

Grammar Point: Chinese doesn't change pronoun forms

Unlike English (I/me, he/him), Chinese pronouns stay the same whether subject or object: 我爱她 (I love her) vs 她爱我 (She loves me).

Number Pattern:

21-99: tens + ones (二十一, 三十五, etc.)
