## Tips for Using These Prompts

1. **Modify as needed:** Adjust ages, locations, or specific details to match your content needs
2. **Add diversity:** Ensure prompts include diverse representation of both students and teachers
3. **Consistency:** Use similar style descriptions for a cohesive look across your website
4. **Quality keywords:** Include "professional photography," "high quality," "natural lighting" for best results
5. **Cultural sensitivity:** Prompts emphasize respect and positive cultural exchange
6. **Modern facilities:** Emphasize well-equipped, modern schools to attract quality teachers

## Style Guidelines

- **Photography style:** Professional, clean, educational
- **Lighting:** Natural, bright, inviting
- **Atmosphere:** Positive, engaging, inspiring
- **Colors:** Vibrant but professional
- **Composition:** Focus on interaction and engagement
- **Quality:** High-resolution, suitable for web and print
