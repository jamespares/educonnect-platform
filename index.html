Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about teaching in China

How much can I earn teaching in China?

Our teaching positions offer 25,000-40,000 RMB monthly (approximately ¬£3,000-¬£4,800), plus benefits including housing assistance, medical insurance, and paid holidays. Many teachers save 60-70% of their salary.

What qualifications do I need to teach in China?

You need a Bachelor's degree, at least one year of teaching experience, a teaching qualification (TEFL/PGCE), and to be a native English speaker from an English-speaking country.

Which cities in China offer the best teaching opportunities?

Shanghai, Beijing, Shenzhen, and Guangzhou offer the highest salaries and most international schools. We also place teachers in Nanjing, Hangzhou, and other tier-1 cities with excellent opportunities.

Do you help with visa applications for China?

Yes, we provide complete visa support including Z-visa applications, work permit assistance, and guidance through all required documentation and legalization processes.

What subjects are most in demand in Chinese international schools?

English, Mathematics, Science, and Physical Education are in highest demand. We also regularly place Art, Music, History, and Computer Science teachers in top international schools.

Is it difficult to adapt to living in China as a foreign teacher?

We provide comprehensive support including free Mandarin lessons, expat community connections, and ongoing assistance to help you settle in. Most teachers find the experience rewarding and exciting.
